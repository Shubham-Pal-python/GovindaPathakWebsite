About Us

Our Govinda Pathak was founded in **2023** with a passion for preserving the vibrant tradition of Dahi Handi and fostering a strong sense of community. Though young, our spirit is boundless, and our commitment to discipline, teamwork, and safety is unwavering.

We believe Dahi Handi is more than just breaking a pot; it's about building human pyramids, trusting your fellow Govindas, and celebrating the joyous spirit of Krishna Janmashtami together. We focus on rigorous practice, physical conditioning, and ensuring the safety of all our participants.

Join us as we continue to grow, strive for new heights, and spread the joy of this incredible festival!

Join Our Pathak!

Are you ready to be a part of the Govinda spirit? We welcome enthusiastic new members to join our family!

Why Join Our Govinda Pathak?

  • Experience the thrill of Dahi Handi with a dedicated team.
  • Learn techniques for safe and effective human pyramid formation.
  • Improve your physical fitness and agility.
  • Forge lifelong friendships and be part of a vibrant community.
  • Contribute to preserving a cherished cultural tradition.
  • Emphasis on safety, discipline, and teamwork.

Important Note: Please ensure you are medically fit for strenuous physical activity. We prioritize the safety of all our Govindas.
